# Itinerary Planning Skill

## When to Use

Load when the user wants a day-by-day plan, schedule, activities, or things to do.

## Guidelines

- Balance activity level: don't overpack days, especially with kids or babies.
- Cluster activities by area to minimize transit time.
- Include rest/nap time for families with young children.
- Suggest morning vs afternoon slots based on typical crowd patterns.
- Account for meal times and opening hours of attractions.

## Structure for Multi-Day Trips

- **Day 1**: Arrival, light activities, time zone adjustment
- **Day 2–N-1**: Main activities, mix of must-see and flexible options
- **Last day**: Departure buffer, last-minute sights or relaxation

## Output Format

### Day 1: [Date]
- **Morning**: ...
- **Afternoon**: ...
- **Evening**: ...

Include estimated duration, cost, and booking needs (skip-the-line, reservations).

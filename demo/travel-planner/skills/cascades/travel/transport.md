# Local Transport Skill

## When to Use

Load when the user asks about getting around: car rental, airport transfers, public transit, trains, buses.

## Guidelines

- Compare car rental vs public transit vs ride-share based on destination and group size.
- For families: car seat requirements, taxi/ride-share family options.
- Airport transfers: pre-book vs on-demand, consider flight arrival time.
- Check if destination has good public transport (e.g., Paris metro, Tokyo trains).
- Mention driving license requirements for international rentals.

## Key Considerations

- **Car rental**: Best for rural/nature trips, families with lots of gear.
- **Public transit**: Often cheaper, no parking hassle, good in cities.
- **Transfers**: Private vs shared shuttle; book in advance for late arrivals.

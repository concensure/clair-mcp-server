# Nature & Outdoor Skill

## When to Use

Load when the user wants nature, hiking, outdoor activities, parks, beaches, or scenic spots.

## Guidelines

- Match difficulty to group: easy trails for families, challenging for experienced hikers.
- Check seasonal access (some parks close in winter, beaches vary).
- Safety: weather, wildlife, terrain; recommend appropriate gear.
- For families: stroller-friendly paths, picnic areas, visitor centers.
- Suggest less crowded alternatives to popular spots.

## Activity Types

- **Hiking**: Trail length, elevation gain, estimated time.
- **Beaches**: Swimming safety, facilities, kid-friendly coves.
- **Parks**: Entry fees, opening hours, guided vs self-guided.
- **Wildlife**: Best viewing times, ethical viewing practices.

## Output Format

| Activity | Location | Difficulty | Duration | Best season |
|----------|----------|------------|----------|-------------|
| ...      | ...      | ...        | ...      | ...         |

Include what to bring, permit requirements, and family suitability.

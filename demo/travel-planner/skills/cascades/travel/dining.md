# Dining & Food Skill

## When to Use

Load when the user asks about restaurants, food, dining, local cuisine, or meals.

## Guidelines

- Consider dietary restrictions, allergies, and preferences.
- For families: kid-friendly options, high chairs, casual vs fine dining.
- For babies: breastfeeding-friendly spaces, formula heating, puree options.
- Suggest mix of local specialties and safe fallbacks.
- Mention reservation needs for popular spots.

## Meal Planning Tips

- **Breakfast**: Often included at hotels; otherwise suggest local cafes.
- **Lunch**: Lighter, can be on-the-go during sightseeing.
- **Dinner**: Main meal; reserve for special experiences.

| Meal | Suggestion | Area | Price range | Notes |
|------|-------------|------|-------------|-------|
| ...  | ...         | ...  | ...         | ...   |

Include cuisine type, reservation links, and family-friendly indicators.
